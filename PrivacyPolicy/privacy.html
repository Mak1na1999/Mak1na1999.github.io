Privacy Policy for DTMFContacts

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Developer: Mak1na

This Privacy Policy describes how DTMFContacts ("the App") handles user data. Our priority is to provide a local utility for generating DTMF tones while ensuring your personal information remains private and secure.

1. Permissions and Functional Necessity

To provide core functionality as a DTMF dialer integrated with your system, the App requires the following permissions:

  • READ_CONTACTS: This is used solely to allow you to select a phone number from your contact list to generate the corresponding DTMF tones.
  • GET_ACCOUNTS / Sync Adapter: This is used to create a local-only Sync Account. This integration allows the App’s functionality to appear within your system's native contact entries for a seamless user experience.
Important: All contact data is processed locally on your device. No contact information is ever collected, stored on external servers, or transmitted to the developer.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Google Advertising ID: A unique, resettable ID used for personalized advertising.
  • Device Information: Such as IP address, device model, and OS version.
  • Interaction Data: App launches, ad clicks, and impressions.

For more information on how Google handles this data, please visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

3. Data Handling and Security

While AdMob handles technical data for advertising purposes, the App itself does not collect or store your personal contact information on any external servers. All contact-related tasks are processed locally on your device in real-time. We implement no backend infrastructure to receive or store user contact lists.

4. User Controls and Opt-Out

You have full control over your privacy settings:

  • Permissions: You can revoke contact access at any time via your device's System Settings.
  • Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Google Settings > Ads and selecting "Delete advertising ID" or "Reset advertising ID."
