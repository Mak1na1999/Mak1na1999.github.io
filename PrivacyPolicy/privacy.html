Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mak1na (Author of the App) handles data in the DTMFContacts mobile application.

1. Permissions and Sync Adapter

To provide core functionality as a DTMF dialer integrated with your system, our app requires:

  • GET_ACCOUNTS: To create a local Sync Account so our app icon appears in your system contacts.
  • READ_CONTACTS: To allow you to pick numbers for tone generation.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve relevant ads to you, including:

  • Google Advertising ID: A unique, resettable ID used for personalized advertising.
  • Device Information: Such as IP address, device model, and OS version.
  • Interaction Data: App launches, ad clicks, and impressions.

For more information, please visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

3. Data Handling and Security

While AdMob handles data for advertising, our app does not collect or store your personal contact information on any external servers. All contact-related tasks are processed locally on your device We access contacts locally to generate tones; no contact data is collected or stored by the developer, though third-party ads may collect device and approximate location data for advertising purposes.

4. Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Google Settings > Ads and selecting "Delete advertising ID" or "Reset advertising ID."
