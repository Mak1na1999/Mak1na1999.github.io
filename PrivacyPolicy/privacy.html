Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mak1na ("we", "us", or "our") handles data in the DTMFContacts mobile application for Android.

1. Permissions and Data Access

To provide core functionality, our app requires access to specific system permissions. We handle this data with strict privacy in mind:

  • GET_ACCOUNTS: Used to manage a local Sync Account. This is required to integrate our app's shortcut icons into your system's native Contacts app.
  • READ_CONTACTS: Allows you to select a contact number to generate DTMF tones.
  • WRITE_CONTACTS & Sync Settings: Used to link our service with your existing contact cards for quick access.

2. Local Processing (No Data Collection)

We believe in "Privacy by Design." Our app does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to external servers.

  • All contact processing and account management happen entirely on your device.
  • No contact lists, account names, or phone numbers are ever uploaded or shared with us or any third parties.
  • We do not track your location or record your audio during DTMF tone generation.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs that harvest user data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately once posted.
